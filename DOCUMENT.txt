SECTIONS.
1. Hero / Introduction
What it is: Your name, a clear professional title (e.g., "Full-Stack Developer"), 
and a brief one-sentence hook about what you build.Placement: Top of the page (above the fold).

2. About MeWhat it is: A short bio highlighting your tech passions, career goals, 
and what makes you unique.Placement: Immediately below the introduction.

3. Technical SkillsWhat it is: A scannable list of your core technologies, languages, 
and frameworks. Group them logically (e.g., Frontend, Backend, Databases).Placement: 
Right after the 'About Me' or sidebar.

4. Projects (The Core)What it is: 3-4 high-quality projects you are proudest of. 
For each, include the name, tech stack, a summary of the problem solved, and links to 
the GitHub and live demo.Placement: Prominently in the center of the page.

5. Work Experience / Case StudiesWhat it is: A selective timeline of your professional roles, 
internships, or detailed project case studies. Focus heavily on technical impact.Placement: 
Below the projects section.

6. Education & Certifications What it is: Degrees, bootcamps, or relevant technical certificates. 
Keep it concise.Placement: Near the bottom, before contact details.

7. Contact & LinksWhat it is: Your email, a contact form, and links to professional profiles like
 GitHub or LinkedIn.Placement: In the footer, and optionally a fixed navigation bar.
